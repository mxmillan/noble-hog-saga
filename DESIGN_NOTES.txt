NOBLE HOG SAGA — DESIGN NOTES

Generated: 2026-02-28


LEVEL DESIGN ISSUES
-------------------

Zone 1 is effectively flat.
  The elevated wood plank platforms are optional — a player who just runs
  left-to-right on the ground loses almost nothing. There's no incentive
  to go up and no punishment for ignoring them. The first 2000px should
  force some vertical engagement.


Zone boundary mismatch.
  The visual zone transitions (getZone()) trigger at x=1800, 4000, 6300
  but the platform layout is commented as 2000, 4200, 6500. The sky and
  background shift 200px before the platform type changes.



ASSET INVENTORY
---------------

The level1_tiles/ folder is completely empty. Every platform and
background layer is currently procedural fallback (solid colours).
That is the single biggest visual gap in the game right now.

ASSET FORMAT RULES
-------------------

- PNG only (SpriteLoader only loads .png files)
- No fixed minimum resolution — blit() stretches to fit, so pixel art
  at 2x or 4x native scales fine
- Tiles that repeat horizontally MUST have matching left and right edges
- Background layers also tile horizontally — same seamless requirement
- Alpha / transparency is fully supported
- Vertical edges of tiles do not need to match (tiles are anchored at
  the bottom of each platform, not stacked)
